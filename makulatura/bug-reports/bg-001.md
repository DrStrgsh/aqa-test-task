# BG-001 Mobile: Menu modal is overlapping 'Login' modal

## Description:
- Menu modal is not closing after clicking 'Login' button in Mobile web view and login modal is overlapped by menu modal.

## Severity:
- **Major**

## Environments:
- Mobile browsers (Pixel 5 & iPhone 12)

## Preconditions:
- User is logged out
- User is located on main page (https://staging-stack.alynea.io/)

Steps:
1. Click **'Hamburger'** icon on the top right corner of the page
2. Click **'Login'** button

## Expected result:
- **Menu Modal** is closed and **Login Modal** form is displayed

## Actual result:
- **Menu Modal** remains open and overlaps **'Login'** modal
